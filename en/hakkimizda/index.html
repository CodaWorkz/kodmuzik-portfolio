ABOUT

KOD Müzik has programmed and produced concerts and special series since the late 1990s, working with public institutions, festivals and venues across Turkey and beyond. Projects have been staged at CRR Concert Hall, İş Sanat, Zorlu PSM, CSO Ada Ankara, Borusan Music House, Harbiye Cemil Topuzlu Open-Air Theatre, Volkswagen Arena and Dubai Opera House, among others.

The program spans contemporary jazz, experimental music, post-rock, progressive rock, electronic and world traditions. Selected appearances include Chick Corea, John McLaughlin & The 4th Dimension, Wayne Shorter Quartet, Jan Garbarek (with Trilok Gurtu), Brad Mehldau Trio, Marcus Miller, Kronos Quartet, The Bad Plus, Hiromi, Jethro Tull, Dream Theater, Joe Satriani, Soft Machine, Swans, Godspeed You! Black Emperor, Tortoise, The Necks, Stereolab, Mouse on Mars, GAS (Wolfgang Voigt) with Ali Demirel, The Bug & Flowdan, Michael Rother (plays Neu!/Harmonia), Sona Jobarteh, Rabih Abou-Khalil feat. Elina Duni, Eivør and many others. Ongoing work includes the Nova Muzak series at Borusan Music House, which has hosted artists such as Senyawa, James Brandon Lewis Trio, [Ahmed] and Hackedepicciotto with Rafael Toral.

KOD's role on each project varies by need—concert production, artist coordination, venue and stage solutions, and musical/creative consulting—delivered in collaboration with presenting partners. The site documents this body of work from 1999 to today.
